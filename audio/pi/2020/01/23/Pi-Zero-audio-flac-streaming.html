Pi Zero Audio Flac Streaming

Jan 23, 2020

WORK IN PROGRESS - NOTHING HERE QUITE YET

I’ve been looking for cheap HW solution to stream audio to a DAC I relocated far awayfrom my computer.

All solutions I found were either too expensive or making compromises on quality. My Maverick Audio DAC has an toslink input that was previously connected to the digital output of my computer.

I thought about buying an 2e generation Apple TV (with toslink output) but streaming to it using Airplay is limited to 44kHz/16 bit streams… The WR320 Wireless WIFI Audio Receiver seems to be a good and cheap solution at 56€. It has an optical out but it seems to require some specific apps for configuration and streaming…(amazon) same for the Yamaha AWXAD10DGY at 124€ (amazon)

At the end I decided to order a Raspberry Pi Zero W and a JustBoom Digi Zero pHAT. At about 62€ (including a 16GB microsd), this solution is more expensive than the WR320 but I will be able to recycle this hardware to other uses if needed.
